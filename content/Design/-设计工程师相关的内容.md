#### README.md

Hi, I'm Flo. I'm a designer turned design engineer. I didn't code much at all until November of 2024. [v0](https://v0.dev/) introduced me into vibe-coding, which got me building [personal projects](https://github.com/floguo), which lead me to joining [Vercel](https://vercel.com/). At Vercel, I worked on back-of-frontend and interactions for v0. Now, I work on agentic interfaces & workflows as the first founding design engineer at [Paradigm](https://www.floguo.com/notes/paradigmai.com).  
大家好，我是 Flo。我曾是一名设计师，后来转型成为设计工程师。直到 2024 年 11 月，我才开始接触编程 [。v0](https://v0.dev/) 让我接触到了 vibe-coding，并由此开始构建 [个人项目](https://github.com/floguo) ，最终加入了 [Vercel](https://vercel.com/) 。在 Vercel，我负责 v0 的后端和交互设计。现在，作为 [Paradigm](https://www.floguo.com/notes/paradigmai.com) 的首位创始设计工程师，我致力于智能体界面和工作流的研究。

## Notes 笔记

**Material understanding beats tool proficiency.** Only mastering Figma and React misses the point. Design engineers think in terms of the final medium: what users actually interact with. Code and pixels become one malleable material.  
**对材料的理解胜过对工具的熟练掌握。** 仅仅精通 Figma 和 React 并不能说明问题。设计工程师思考的是最终媒介：用户实际与之交互的内容。代码和像素最终会融合为一种可塑性极强的材料。

**Design engineering streamlines the creation process, minimizing back-and-forth.** Marrying design and implementation internalizes the translation layer, rather than externalizing it into handoffs. Time collapses between idea and validation.  
**设计工程简化了创建流程，最大限度地减少了反复沟通。** 将设计与实现相结合，使转换层内部化，而不是将其外包给不同的部门或人员。从构思到验证所需的时间大大缩短。

**Prototyping is central but not the whole story.** While rapid prototyping and UI implementation are visible outputs, the deeper value is in establishing strong foundations—design systems, component libraries, and patterns that elevate entire teams.  
**原型设计固然重要，但并非全部。** 快速原型设计和用户界面实现虽然是显而易见的成果，但其更深层的价值在于建立强大的基础——设计系统、组件库和模式，这些都能提升整个团队的水平。
